Scourge Invasion: Icecrown Rares (EU)

This site shows the Icecrown rare rotation for the Scourge Invasion.
It sorts the rares in order of appearance and shows the time remaining until spawn, as well as the spawn time in your local time.

Multiple rares can be up if they are not killed, this page shows the latest spawn. Previous spawns are last in the rotation and can be checked at the end of the list.
